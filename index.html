Electronics for Mechanical Engineers

Electronics for Mechanical Engineers is a class developed by Brandon Stafford, first taught in the spring of 2018 at Tufts University.

The point of the class is to provide you with hands-on experience building and debugging electronics, to complement the theory that you learned in ES3, ME18, and so forth. To that end, the class is based around a series of projects, which range from designing a basic regulated power supply to building a high-current motor controller. The final project is one of your own design.
